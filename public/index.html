        How this works

        Agent 11 doesn't persist guidelines on the server — this deployment runs as a stateless serverless function. Whatever you upload here stays in your browser session and is resent automatically with every generation request in Content Studio. Refreshing the page clears it.

        Campaigns

        Every post generated in Content Studio can be published immediately from its result card — that part works today. Multi-post campaign scheduling needs a persistent background worker to fire posts at a future time, which a stateless serverless deployment (this one) doesn't have by design.

        To enable scheduled campaigns: add a queue and worker (e.g. Vercel Cron plus a small always-on worker, or a dedicated task queue) that calls /api/publish at the scheduled time. Until then, use "Post now" on any generated option.

        Analytics

        No analytics data source is connected yet. This view will populate once posts are tracked somewhere durable — e.g. storing publish results in a database, or pulling engagement metrics back from each platform's own API. Nothing is fabricated here in the meantime.
